## Effect

Inflicts regular damage.  Power increases with the target's weight in kilograms, to a maximum of 120.

Target's weight | Power
--------------- | ----:
Up to 10kg      |    20
Up to 25kg      |    40
Up to 50kg      |    60
Up to 100kg     |    80
Up to 200kg     |   100
Above 200kg     |   120
